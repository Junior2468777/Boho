Get to Know Us

Boho Bistro is a themed, unique, and high-energy restaurant, bar, and lounge, perfect for those who enjoy delectable cuisine, trendy music, and live entertainment.

We are one of the latest establishments to join the Bloemfontein social scene with bright, colorful, and bohemian-inspired designs, providing an ultimate comfortable atmosphere for locals and tourists from different nationalities and cultural backgrounds.
